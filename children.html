Psychological problems of children
Mental disorders in children and adolescents are more
common and more serious than we think. It is estimated
that 10-20% of adolescents worldwide struggle with
mental illness. Unfortunately, most go unnoticed, and
many adolescent mental illnesses get worse in adulthood
because they go undiagnosed and untreated.
Problem classification
In the medical dictionary, common psychological problems in children
are adjustment disorder of mood and anxiety (triggered by stress), major
depression, anxiety disorder, panic disorder, eating disorder, social anxiety,
attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), learning disability disorder,
school refusal, autism spectrum disorder (ASD), oppositional defiance
disorder; Behavioral disorders and gaming/Internet addiction.
Solution
l Don't ignore problems, focus on them first.
l Try open communication with your child.
l Take your child to a medical professional.
l Create a safe and comfortable home environment.
l Try to eliminate unnecessary stress from their lives.
l Talk to their schools and teachers.
