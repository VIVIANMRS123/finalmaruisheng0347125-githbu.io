Adolescent psychological problems
According to a survey, more than 20 percent of
China's youth have mental health problems of varying
degrees, said Guo Cheng, a professor at Southwest
University and a national training expert on mental health education under the Ministry of Education
Problem classification
Mainly include depression, anxiety,
social maladjustment, self-awareness is not sound.
Depression is one of the most common psychological
problems in adolescents.
Solution
Families, schools, society and
other parties should pay extensive attention
t o and participate in order to fasten the "safety belt" of
adolescent mental health.
