Psychological problems of the elderly
With the decline of physiological function and
the influence of various family and social factors, elderly people are under greater psychological and
physiological pressure than young people. Therefore, in their later years, they may experience various
psychological and mental problems such as anxiety, depression, and insomnia, which seriously affect
their quality of life. Therefore, in addition to paying attention to physical health, elderly health and wellness
also urgently need to supplement "psychological nutrition".
Problem classification
1) Elderly Sleep disorder
2) Elderly depression
3) Elderly anxiety disorder
4) Senile Hypochondriasis
5) Elderly mental disorders: suspected surveillance,
6) suspected harm, suspected wife or husband having an affair, behavioral disorders, or various hallucinations, delusions, etc.
Solution
1) Live to be old, learn to be old, study diligently, use the brain scientifically
2) Participate more in social activities and readjust to society
3) Exercise more, listen to more music, and read more
4) Maintain an optimistic and tolerant attitude
5) Cultivate various hobbies
